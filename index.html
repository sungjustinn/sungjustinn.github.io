About This Page

In this website, I created a database of hip hop dance competitions.
The data would include information such as the competition name, the YouTube video link, the year of the competition, the dance team, the songs within the set, and the placing.
I hope to have others who are more knowledgeable about these competitions to help me fill in information
and to make this a collaborative effort. Hope you enjoy!
